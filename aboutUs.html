How and where it all started...

In the year 2000 our CEOs Jones Schmidt and Aleyna Russel yearned to find a Hotel in Ireland which would satisfie all of their needs: a good location, helpful and nice servive, affordability, cleanliness, relaxation, and safety. So they took the matter into their own hands and, as a romantic couple themselves, established Safescape - a Hotel that would meet the needs of many other romantic couples, as well as solo travelers searching for a safe and warm experience. Originally starting of in Dublin - the heart of Ireland - Safescape is now also operating in Berlin, Germany and London, United Kingdom. With three Europe-based Hotels in total, our CEOs and team now focus on perfecting our Wellness-Business, before exloring more locations in other beautiful countries.

Aleyna Russel & Jones Schmidt
