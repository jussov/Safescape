How do I Ckeck-In?

Online

After booking your stay at our hotel, you receive a confirmation email. In this email you wil find a "Check-In Now!" button. Click this button to confirm you identity. After doing so, we will send you another email confirming your Check-In. Collect your room card from our Front Desk Team.

Reception

For a Check-In on site, please talk to our team at the Front Desk to inform them about your arrival.

How do I Check-Out?

In our Lobby you can find multiple touchscreens. To Check-Out, please insert your room card into the "Room Card" slot and click on "Check-Out" when it pops up on the touchscreen. After the system is done processing the card, take out the room card and put it into the small box next to the "Room Card" slot saying "Return".
